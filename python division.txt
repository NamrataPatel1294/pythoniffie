 USE THE DIVISION OPERATOR TO DIVIDE
Use the division operator (/) to divide two numbers and return their quotient as a float.

quotient = 5 / 2
Exact division

print(quotient)
OUTPUT
2.5
USE THE INTEGER DIVISION OPERATOR TO DIVIDE
Use the integer division operator (//) to divide two numbers and round their quotient down to nearest integer.

quotient = 5 // 2
Integer division

print(quotient)
OUTPUT
2
